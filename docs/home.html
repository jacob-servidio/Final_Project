Story

The purpose of this project was to create a site to help long-term traders create forecasts and positions for stocks. As a proof of concept, we focused on FedEx. Our machine learning was focused on quarterly ratios, finding trends, and offering a prediction for the nect quarter. Our candlestick graph focused on the yearly numbers of FedEx's stock pricing. This was to allow managers to gauge the overall stock health for the year as well as the emotion of the market. Also, we are not traders. We are not giving stock tips or advice within this site. Use you own judgement, do some research, and learn on you own. We are not financial advisors, we are merely giving our interpretations of data.
